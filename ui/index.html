Routines · scheduled wake-ups

Active wake-ups across all chats. Cancel any without re-opening its chat. Cancelling kills the process the chat started, removes its crontab line if it had one, and stops the routine from firing again.

Snapshots · time-machine

Save a copy of every chat, memory, routine, and skill. Restore a snapshot to roll the entire bridge back to that moment — workers stop, state swaps, and chats lazy-respawn when you open them. Routines from the snapshot keep their scripts on disk; their PIDs (stale) get marked cancelled until a chat re-registers them.
